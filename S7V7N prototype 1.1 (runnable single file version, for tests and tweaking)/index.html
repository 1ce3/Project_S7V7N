Warranty Policy

To facilitate warranty inquiries for products purchased at S7V7N, we respectfully provide our customers with the following warranty policies and contact information.

1. Product Warranty Contact

When you need warranty service, please contact S7V7N through the following methods:

  • Call the warranty hotline: 1900 3677
  • Send a direct message via our website or fanpage
  • Bring the product directly to S7V7N branches or Warranty Centers
  • Bring the product directly to the manufacturer’s/ supplier’s Warranty Center

Note:

  • Customers may also send products for warranty through a shipping service. After receiving the product, S7V7N will provide feedback regarding warranty information.
  • Please contact our support staff before sending products to receive assistance.

2. Warranty Conditions

  • The product must still be within S7V7N’s warranty period; the warranty period is recorded based on purchase information and the product’s Serial Number.
  • The product must retain the warranty seal or distributor’s seal. For products requiring warranty with the box, customers must send the full box and included accessories.
  • The product must remain intact, without scratches, dents, or deformation beyond the manufacturer’s/distributor’s regulations.
  • The product must have defects arising during use due to manufacturer issues such as components or technical faults.

Note:

  • Specific warranty conditions may vary depending on the manufacturer and supplier.
  • Data stored in products (Laptop/Desktop/Memory Card/Hard Drive…) is not covered under warranty. We are not responsible for any direct or indirect damages caused to customers if data stored in the product is disclosed, lost, or damaged during inspection or warranty processing.
  • Customers should carefully review the warranty card and manufacturer’s warranty policy before using the product.

3. Products Not Eligible for Warranty

  • Products whose S7V7N warranty period has expired.
  • Products missing required devices or accessories (e.g., receiver, adapter…).
  • Products without purchase information or not supplied by S7V7N.
  • Products damaged due to user error (dropping, denting, deformation, water damage, or improper use).
  • Products that have been repaired, disassembled, or structurally altered by unauthorized third parties.
  • Product information (Serial Number/IMEI/Service Tag) is missing or does not match the actual product.
  • Products damaged due to objective reasons such as natural disasters, fire, animals, insects, or environmental wear and tear.

4. General Warranty Policy

All products sold by S7V7N are warranted according to the regulations of the manufacturer/distributor. All defective products will be sent to the manufacturer or an authorized service provider for warranty service. S7V7N is not responsible if the manufacturer or authorized service provider refuses warranty.

4.1 Replacement Policy

Product replacement within 7 days:

For products with defects caused by the manufacturer or S7V7N, customers may request a replacement within 7 days from the purchase date. Applicable to Laptop and Monitor products (except for certain product lines with separate warranty policies).

Product replacement within 30 days:

For products with defects caused by the manufacturer or S7V7N, customers may request a replacement within 30 days from the purchase date. Applicable to gaming gear (keyboard, mouse, headset…) and computer components (NUC, CPU, RAM, Hard Drive, cooling system, power supply, and other devices).

Requirements for replacement products:

  • The product must be in the same condition as when purchased.
  • The box must be like new, without dents, tears, writing, drawing, or tape/glue marks.
  • All included accessories must be intact, undamaged, and scratch-free.

Delivery Policy

S7V7N provides nationwide delivery service, shipping directly to the address provided by customers. The estimated delivery time depends on the warehouse and the customer’s delivery address. For most orders, S7V7N requires a few working hours to verify information and package the products. If all items are available in stock, S7V7N will promptly hand them over to the shipping partner.

  • Working days are from Monday to Friday, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, public holidays, Tet holidays, compensatory days off, and do not include remote island districts.

IMPORTANT NOTES WHEN RECEIVING GOODS

  • Before delivering the goods, S7V7N Technical Support (HTTK) staff or the courier from our delivery partner will contact you via your registered phone number about 3–5 minutes in advance to confirm delivery.
  • For standard delivery orders: if you are unable to receive the package on the first attempt, the courier will try to contact you at least 2 more times (in 2 different delivery shifts) to arrange a suitable delivery time. Please keep your phone available to coordinate with the courier.
  • Upon receiving the goods, please record a video of the unboxing process. If you refuse to accept the goods due to reasons originating from S7V7N (e.g., product not as described, product quality not guaranteed), your order will be returned to us and canceled in the S7V7N system.
  • If the package shows external signs of damage such as tears, dents, wetness, punctures, or broken seals, please carefully check the product inside before accepting. You have the full right to refuse the delivery and notify us via hotline 1900.5301 for assistance.
  • For any changes to products or order cancellations, please inform us as soon as possible via hotline 1900.5301, or chat directly on the S7V7N fanpage or website for support.
